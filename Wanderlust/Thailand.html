BYD Lofts Boutique Hotel

Curving shapes and a highly unusual architecture are the inventive dwellings of Keemala, an all pool villa wonderland nestled well away from the Phuket crowds and overlooking Kamala Village and the Andaman Sea.

Amari Phuket

The Rayavadee nestles discreetly amidst tropical gardens and coconut groves against a backdrop of sheer cliffs, lush jungle foliage and the clear waters of the Andaman Sea. In addition to stylish, five-star accommodation, Rayavadee also boasts fine cuisine, one of the most unique relaxation spas in Thailand.

Le Meridien Phuket Beach Resort

A range of exclusive thatched villas are available here. Some feature ocean views, direct access to the sandy beach, open air bathrooms, private pools and outdoor terraces.The deluxe resort has two restaurants, an infinity pool, a spa, a gym and three bars
